Projects & Research

I have taken part in audio as well as
astronomy research projects!

Human Clarity Research

Designing and training deep learning models using a python module called Northwestern University Source Separation Library (nussl). The objective of the study was to understand how humans hear clarity.

Double-lined Spectroscopic Binaries

I research binary star systems' spectra to uncover if there is a correlation between a star's rotational velocity and its lithium abundance. During the summer of 2019, I made great progress on my research so much so that I concluded the summer by submitting my work for publication in the American Astronomical Society's Research Note Journal.

Nova Search

My job was to teach an incoming freshman how to use particular functions in IRAF(Image Reduction and Analysis Facility) in order to manipulate the images of M31 to work with the Novasearch tool created by Frank Morris. To make repetitive steps such as trimming a FITS (Flexible Image Transport System) I created various Astropy programs. The Novasearch tool is used for astronomy education.

Vsini Machine Learning Model

This model estimates the vsini for single star system given the star's radial velocity graph. My implementation of this can be found here. I created this because there are two was in which a scientist can calculate the full-with half-max of a spectrum.
